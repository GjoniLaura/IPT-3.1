The Origin of Basketball

Basketball was invented by James Naismith, a Canadian physical education teacher and doctor. In 1891 he was to develop a sport that would allow students to exercise during the winter months. Naismith attached two baskets of peach to the ends of the hall and developed 13 rules. On December 21, 1891, the first basketball game took place. The game quickly gained popularity and spread to schools and universities. Improvements were introduced over time, such as the backboard and fairer rules. Basketball became popular worldwide and is one of the most popular sports today.

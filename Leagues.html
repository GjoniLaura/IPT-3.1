NBA

The National Basketball Association is the professional basketball league in North America and was founded in 1946. It is considered by far the strongest and most popular basketball league in the world. The NBA is currently made up of 30 teams, 29 in the United States and one in Canada.

WNBA

The Women's National Basketball Association is the North American professional basketball league for women, founded in 1996, as the sports association is called. The league currently consists of twelve teams, all based in the United States.

NBL

The National Basketball League is an Australian professional basketball league currently made up of ten teams from Australia and New Zealand. It is by far the strongest basketball league in Oceania and is one of the top 20 in the world in an international comparison. The association was founded in Australia in 1979.

WNBL

The Women's National Basketball League is Australia's pre-eminent professional women's basketball league. It currently consists of eight teams. The league was founded in 1981 as the women's division of the National Basketball League. Several WNBL teams have NBL opponents.

FIBA

The International Basketball Federation (FIBA) is the world basketball governing body based in Mies (Switzerland) and was founded in 1932 as the International Basketball Federation (FIBB).

Teams in different leagues

In the fascinating world of basketball, there are a variety of teams competing in different leagues around the world. Each league has its own identity, skill level and history, resulting in exciting competitions and engaging games. Each league offers its own atmosphere, skill level, and rivalries that make the sport of basketball exciting and diverse. Whether in North America, Europe, Asia or anywhere else in the world - basketball fans will find fascinating teams and thrilling games everywhere.
